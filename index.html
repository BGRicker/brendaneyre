Brendan Eyre

About Brendan

Brendan Eyre is one of the country’s top young comedians. He has appeared on Late Night with Seth Meyers, Comedy Central, and Gotham Comedy Live. He is a regular on Sirius XM Satellite radio and was a New Face of Comedy at Montreal’s prestigious Just For Laughs Comedy Festival. Brendan cohosts the popular Rad Dudecast Podcast and headlines clubs throughout the country. He is also a blue belt in jiu jitsu.
